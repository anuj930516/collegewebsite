Admission Procedure & Fee Structure

Candidates are admitted in different Polytechnics through counseling after qualifying the Entrance Examination conducted by Joint Entrance Examination Council ( JEECUP ), Lucknow. All the reservations are effective as per UP government rules. For Courses, Qualification & other details refer to https://www.jeecup.nic.in

Documents Required

Following documents are required for first year students:-

1- Allotment letter generated by jeecup counseling.

2- Fee draft in favor of Principal Government Polytechnic, kanpur.

3- Self attested photocopies of educational qualification certificates such as high school, intermediate, graduation, etc. and other such as adhar card, caste certificate, income certificate and domicile certificate

4- Original transfer certificate, migration certificate, character certificate of last institution and medical fitness certificate from CMO of any government district hospital.

5- Gap certificate.
